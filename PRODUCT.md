# PRODUCT.md

> Strategy context for design work. Read automatically before any design pass.

## Register

**Product surface with brand warmth.** This is a personal tool first (inventory + planning),
but it is also a maker's private studio, so it should feel crafted and warm rather than
neutral and corporate. Design serves the content (yarn photos, projects), with enough
personality to feel like a hobby you love rather than a spreadsheet.

## Who is this for

One maker — a knitter/crocheter cataloguing her yarn stash, planning projects, and sizing
garments. She reaches for it on her phone while shopping for yarn and on a laptop while
planning a sweater. She already knows her materials; the app should make them feel
beautiful and findable, not teach her the craft.

## Brand voice (three words)

**Warm. Crafted. Calm.**

## Visual references

- A sunlit craft studio: oat wool, cream paper, natural light.
- Hand-labeled yarn skeins and fiber-shop swatch cards.
- Soft display serifs (Fraunces) paired with a friendly humanist sans.

## Anti-references

- Cold SaaS dashboards (Stripe/admin gray-on-white).
- Default Bootstrap / unstyled Vite scaffold.
- Neon, high-contrast tech aesthetics.
